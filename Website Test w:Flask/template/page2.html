Italian Original Pizza

"The best places in Italy and what to have"

    Although you don't have to be in Italy to enjoy Italian pizza (although it helps!) it's hard to argue against the fact that sometimes the Italians just do it better. To enjoy what makes Italian pizza so great, outside of the recipie, it's great to have a starting point on some of the better pizza ever made. Here is a list of some pizza to try if you ever find yourself in Italy:

  1. Pepe in Grani, Caiazzo (Campania) - Margherita
  2. Pizzarium, Rome - Potato and Mozzarela
  3. 50 Kalo, Naples- Marinara
  4. I Tigli, Verona- Shrimp Shashimi
  5. Pizzeria Fratelli Salvo, Naples- Cosacca
  6. ...any many more!

While the main home of these pizza may be Italy, it doesn't mean you can't find them elsewhere with similar or even better quality than some of these restaurants. There are many places in the world that do make great quality pizza. That includes here in the West! On the last and final page of this website find out what pizza in America stick out, and where to find them on your next trip around the states!
